About Me

I'm an experienced Digital Product & Experience Designer based in Wellington, New Zealand. I help organisations create valuable products and services through UX research, strategy & design. I strive to always put the end-user at the heart of the design process - applying the full design spectrum to craft digital solutions that users need, whilst helping businesses thrive in the digital world.

I've had the privilege of gaining hands-on experience in a variety of design disciplines. I've been a product designer dedicated to SaaS web applications, a 'jack-of-all' digital designer working on graphic, motion, marketing and interface design, a project manager for web & mobile software delivery and a product owner for a team of developers.

I've worked with a diverse range of clients on some really interesting projects - my set of experiences has meant that no two weeks are ever really the same for me, and I wouldn't have it any other way. I love working in a fast-paced environment with interesting challenges to solve, and great people to collaborate with and learn from.
